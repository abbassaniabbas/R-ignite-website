Company Overview

Who We Are

R-Ignite Solutions Limited is positioned around intelligence, engineering, and innovation.

R-Ignite Solutions Limited is a Nigerian technology company delivering AI, data, software, and emerging technology solutions for forward-looking organisations. We work with businesses, institutions, and public-sector clients that want stronger decision-making, better operations, and digital systems that can scale with real use.

Our direction is broader than a single service line but still focused: practical intelligence systems, software products, research-led emerging technology work, and advisory support for organisations navigating digital change.

Vision

To help African organisations operate with stronger intelligence, better systems, and more confident technology decisions.

We want R-Ignite to be known for thoughtful execution, practical innovation, and systems that make organisations more capable over time.

Mission

To deliver AI, data, software, and emerging technology solutions that solve real operational problems.

We combine analysis, engineering, research, and advisory work to help clients move from manual or fragmented processes to more intelligent, reliable ways of operating.

Industries Served

Our strongest fit is with organisations facing operational complexity, reporting pressure, fragmented systems, or a need to modernise core workflows without losing control. That includes enterprise, institutional, and public-sector environments where technology decisions have strategic weight.
