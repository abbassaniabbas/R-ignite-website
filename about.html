Who We Are

A premium consultancy built from the ground up for Africa's largest economy.

R-Ignite Group is a premium Data, AI & Digital Transformation company serving Nigerian mid-to-large organisations. We specialize in high-value enterprise solutions — not generic tech, but mission-critical systems that drive measurable outcomes.

We operate as a lean, premium consultancy focused on solving complex data and automation challenges. Our hybrid model combines high-ticket B2B services with recurring revenue contracts, creating predictable cash flow and long-term client relationships.

Based in Abuja, we serve banks, government agencies, telecoms, oil & gas companies, and large SMEs — the organisations that power Nigeria's economy.
