Our Goals

The Roadmap to
Market Leadership

Years 0–2 · Stage 1
01
₦100M–₦250M target

Cash Engine

Build a lean, premium B2B service company delivering high-ticket enterprise solutions with fast cash flow. The foundation that funds everything else.

  • Target: ₦100M–₦250M revenue in 24 months
  • Focus: Banks, government, telecoms, oil & gas, large SMEs
  • Core team: 4–5 specialists + strategic outsourcing
  • Services: BI dashboards, AI automation, ERP/CRM, data infrastructure
  • Premium positioning with high-value project delivery
Years 2–4 · Stage 2
02
60%+ recurring revenue

Recurring Revenue

Convert project clients into long-term contracts, creating predictable income and cash flow stability. The engine that creates investor appeal.

  • Transform one-time projects into annual retainers
  • Managed analytics services and AI monitoring contracts
  • Build 60%+ recurring revenue base
  • Reduce dependency on new client acquisition
  • Establish premium brand and long-term client relationships
Years 4–6 · Stage 3
03
10x valuation multiple

Productisation

Identify patterns across engagements and build SaaS products that scale beyond consulting hours. The transformation from service firm to technology company.

  • Launch SaaS products based on repeated client problems
  • Transition to hybrid service + product company
  • Achieve 10x valuation multiple from productisation
  • Create investor-ready business with exit potential
  • Build passive income streams alongside consulting
